Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our products, repair services, support, and orders.

What products does SmartTech Computer Solutions sell?

We sell laptops, desktop computers, printers, routers, flash drives, RAM, keyboards, mice, and other computer accessories.

Do you repair both laptops and desktop computers?

Yes. We handle hardware troubleshooting, screen replacement, keyboard issues, battery replacement, software errors, upgrades, and general maintenance.

Can you set up office networks and Wi-Fi?

Yes. We provide wired and wireless network installation for homes, offices, schools, and small businesses.

Do you install operating systems and software?

Yes. We install operating systems, drivers, antivirus tools, business software, and productivity applications.

How long does a typical repair take?

Minor software issues can often be completed the same day. Hardware repairs depend on the problem and part availability.

Do you provide technical support after purchase?

Yes. Our team provides guidance, setup help, troubleshooting, and after-sales support for products and services purchased from us.
